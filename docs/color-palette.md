# Hemes: Travel App – Color Palette

The Hermes color palette is inspired by the aesthetics of Ancient Greece, reflecting elegance, mythology, architecture, and classical art. The selected colors evoke ceramic art, marble temples, the Aegean Sea, and terracotta elements commonly found in Greek history.

This palette reinforces the brand identity of Hermes by combining strength, sophistication, and cultural depth.

---
